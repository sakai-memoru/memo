## Usage

### normal mode

#### operation

|key      |operation            |
|---------|---------------------|
|x        |delete charactor     |
|r        |replace charactor    |
|R        |replace charactor    |
|~        |upper/lowercase charactor|
|c<motion>|cw                   |
|C        |c$                   |
|d<motion>|dw, d$               |
|D        |d$                   |
|ia       |insert,add           |
|IA       |insert first,add last|
|s        |delete and insert    |
|yy       |yank line            |
|dd       |delete line          |
|cc       |delete line and insert  |
|S        |delete line and insert  |
|J        |join line            |
|oO       |insert line          |
|pP       |paste                |
|---------|---------------------|
|.        |repeat               |
|u,ctrl+Z |undo                 |


#### jump
|key  |operation            |
|-----|---------------------|
|hjkl |move cursor          |
|wW   |move forward word    |
|eE   |move forward word    |
|bB   |move back word       |
|]}   |move forward block   |
|[{   |move back block      |
|gg   |jump to top          |
|G    |jump to bottom       |
|*    |jump to word above cursor |
|/hoge|jump to search word  |
|n/N  |jump next word       |

#### command

|key  |operation       |
|-----|----------------|
|:w   |write           |
|:q   |quit            |
|:e   |edit other file |

#### scroll
|key  |operation            |
|-----|---------------------|
|HML  |windows high/middle/low   |
|ctrl+u/d |windows up/down       |
